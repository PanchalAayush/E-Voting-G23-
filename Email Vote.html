How to Vote by Mail

All registered voters can vote by mail, also called absentee voting. You can ask to get your ballot in the mail for the next election, or for all future elections.

Request a Ballot

You can request an absentee ballot for yourself, or have a family member request an absentee ballot for you by filling out an application. For the 2020 presidential primaries, you have until 14th Feb at 1:00 PM to turn in your application.
